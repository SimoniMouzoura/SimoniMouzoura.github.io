Project looks at U.K cultural diversity and government expenditure on education. The aim is to see the effect of such expenditure and at the same time explore cultural diversity within the United Kingdom.

Cultural Diversity is the existence of a variety of cultural groups within a society. Such groups can share many different characteristics. They include: culture, religion, ethnicity, language, nationality, sexual orientation, class, gender, age, disability, health differences, geographic location and lots of other things. Several measures of diversity exist and have been repeatedly explored in literature. Such measures include the Ethnic Fractionalisation Index with several other divisions including the Ethnolingusitic Fr. Index.
