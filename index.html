Week 8 - Talking Economics

During one of the "Talking Economics" sessions, Abi Adams Prassl stated that prior to the COVID19 lockdown "U.K had record high employment". Using data from the ONS, I created a chart showing that just before lockdown was imposed in February 2020, UK employment was estimated at record high levels of 76.6%.
